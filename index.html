Upgrading & materials
⛏ Mining efficiency setup
Upgrade the Mining Knuckle Drill to rank 6+. This unlocks Expert Mining, +20% ore yield, and auto-loot. Stack Blessing of the Earth abyss gear on a dedicated farming set if you want a resource run setup.
🐍 Aeserion Scales
Head to the small island in the south. Drop down from an Abyss teleporter above. Grab scales with Axiom Force and store them.
📦 Bag quests after 240 slots
Once inventory is capped, medium bag quests can still pay out useful materials instead of space. Still worth doing.

Chase / future notes
⚫ Fated Shadow
Behind the boss in the Nest of Valor path tied to Unwavering Steps. Keep this noted because the Gray Soul Howling skill is too good to forget.
🎨 Weapon replacement rule
Stop over-refining ugly or weak-looking bases. Replace first, then refine. Core setup matters, but if you hate the look, it will annoy you every time you play.
Armor rule: you already solved the structure. Helmet = Vigor. Chest = Vigor + Momentum + flex. Gloves and boots = movement + vitality. Future changes are mostly better base stats, looks, or fight-specific swaps.

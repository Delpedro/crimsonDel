The most involved earring to get — but the quest chain is worth it and the fight is good practice for positioning.

Quest chain to unlock

  1. 1Unlock Halden: Free Velcom Outpost → return to Hernand → sleep → find the bounty flyer on the wall → go grab him.
  2. 2Defeat Gwen → travel to Lioncrest Manor entrance → the legendary hunter NPC appears.
  3. 3Give hunter 2 leather (instant 100 trust) → give next NPC 2 wine for his info.
  4. 4Go to marked location → examine bodies → track with lantern → defeat Black Lion + pack → skin the beast.
Multi-enemy fight — bring healing. The lion fight tests positioning and stamina. Move speed from your other gear helps.
Upgrading & materials
⛏ Mining efficiency setup
Upgrade the Mining Knuckle Drill to rank 6+ → unlocks Expert Mining, +20% ore yield, and auto-loot (no chasing drops). Stack Blessing of the Earth abyss gear on a dedicated farming set (up to 3 pieces × 30% = 90%). Combined with the drill: 100% bonus ore. Same logic applies to skinning and wood cutting pieces.
🐍 Aeserion Scales (final rank unlock)
Head to the small island in the south. Drop down from any Abyss teleporter above. Grab scales with Axiom Force — store in the Cuckoo Pot. No combat required (serpent dragon didn't aggro). Feels like enough to cover multiple playthroughs.
📦 Don't skip medium bag quests after hitting 240 slots
Once you hit the inventory cap, bag quests stop giving storage — but they now reward valuable materials (Flawless Timber etc.). Quick deliveries, worth doing.
